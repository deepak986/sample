Every skin and every hide is unique its pores and gain lend it its own very special character

Only smooth, open pore leathers are used, the properties of which in each case determine the texture of their surface.

All leathers come form animals reared for meat production in Southern Germany, France, South India and New Zealand

The bags are made of the finest leather not only on the outside, but throughout the inside as well. Their blue lining is a feature of all TSASATA products.

WITH THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE TSASATA, THE TWO HAVE RETURNED TO THEIR FAMILY ROOTS.

A THOUSAND-YEAR-OLD CRAFT

The skins and hides are tanned to produce leather. This craft has been practiced for millennia and today it combines old traditions with cutting-edge, environmentally friendly processes.

All used leathers are free of AZO dyestuffs. PCP and chrome VI

All the tannaries with which TSASATA cooperates fulfill the highest ecological and technical standards. The skins are chosen very carefully.
